STRATEGY Why we do this
1. Premium signal
599 DKK/mo = anchor price. No one pays it. Makes the partner price feel like a deal.
2. Exclusivity
"50% off because you're a [Partner] member." Not a discount — an exclusive deal.
3. Dependency
2 month free trial → integrated in workflow → going back is not an option.
Bottom line: 70-80% of market via partners. 599 DKK exists to make 300 DKK feel like a privilege. Partners get kickback. Users get premium product at exclusive price.

STEP 9 — DECISIONS Contract lifecycle

Scenario 1: Contract expires, NOT renewed

200 doctors on 300 DKK/mo. Contract not renewed. What happens?

Full price immediately

All → 599 DKK. Churn bomb risk.

Grandfather

Existing keep 300. New pay 599. Complex.

Transition period Rec.

3mo notice → gradual increase → full price.

Scenario 2: Contract renegotiated — discount changes

Discount changes from 50% to 30%. Existing pay 300, new price 419. Apply to all?

Grandfather

Existing keep old price. New get new price. Two tiers per partner.

Everyone follows contract

All updated. Simple but price increase.

System req: Contract changes (kickback, discount, expiry) auto-update in system. Stripe charges correct price, kickback adjusts. No manual changes per user.
